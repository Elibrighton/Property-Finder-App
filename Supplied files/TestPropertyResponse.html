18 Callitris Street Acacia Ridge

Subject to the estate!

If you have been looking for a home with potential, that has side access and sits on an elevated large allotment, then your search could well be over. Brick and tile and solidly constructed, the property offers three generous sized rooms all with built-ins, while the master bedroom has an ensuite. Internally there are two living areas, a smaller dining/2nd living and a spacious lounge which has a unique feature wall. Near new air conditioners in the smaller living area and master bedroom ensure your every comfort.

Externally, the property has an electric double garage and a private rear patio, this is the perfect place to keep an eye on the kids/pets playing in the oversized back yard.
Features include;
Fans throughout
Screens throughout
New carpet
Rear storage shed

Located in one of Acacia Ridge's best pockets and on 1055m2 of prime land, this property represents tremendous value. The family's instructions are clear, SELL!
